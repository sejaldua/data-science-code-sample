'''Omega''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Publication history==
Omega first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' #131 (February 1973), and was created by [[Roy Thomas]] and [[Ross Andru]].

The character subsequently appears in ''Marvel Team-Up'' #11 (July 1973), ''Avengers'' #127 (September 1974), ''Fantastic Four'' #150 (September 1974), and ''Avengers'' #20 (September 1999).

==Fictional character biography==
Omega is a gigantic humanoid creation of [[Maximus (comics)|Maximus]] which draws upon psionic power from the racial hatred of the [[Inhumans]] towards the [[Alpha Primitive]]s, and converts that power into strength.{{Issue|date=April 2010}}

Later, [[Ultron]] betrayed Maximus and placed his computer-mind within an enlarged adamantium head and transplanted it atop Omega's body.  In this form, Ultron attacked the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]], [[Fantastic Four]], and Inhumans.<ref>''Avengers'' vol. 1 #127</ref> This continued until it was shut down by [[Franklin Richards (Fantastic Four)|Franklin Richards]].{{Issue|date=April 2010}}
