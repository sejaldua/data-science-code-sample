'''Diamanda Nero''' is a fictional comic book supervillain appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
She was created by [[John Francis Moore (comics)|John Francis Moore]] and [[Pascal Alixe]]. She debuted appeared in the first issue of the mini-series ''X-Men: Phoenix'', which depicts the early battles of [[Rachel Summers]] after arriving to the [[Askani]] timeline.

==Fictional character biography==
In this future, Nero serves as [[Apocalypse (comics)|Apocalypse]]'s second-in-command and the ruler of his empire while the mutant overlord hibernates due to his extremely advanced age.{{issue|date=February 2011}}

According to the ''Official Handbook to the Marvel Universe - Alternate Universes'', Diamanda is Apocalypse's daughter.
