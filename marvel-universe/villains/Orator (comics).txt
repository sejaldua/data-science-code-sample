'''Orator (Victor Ludwig)''' was a minor [[fictional character]] from [[Marvel Comics]].

==Fictional character biography==
He was a [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] who was recruited into the [[Acolytes (comics)|Acolytes]] by [[Exodus (comics)|Exodus]]. He first appeared when the Acolytes were searching for their missing leader [[Magneto (comics)|Magneto]], who was also presumed dead at the time. Instead, they found [[Joseph (comics)|Joseph]], his [[Clone (genetics)|clone]], and attempted but failed to turn him into their new leader.<ref>''Magneto'' #1</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Emotion enhancement; heightened empathetic awareness.
