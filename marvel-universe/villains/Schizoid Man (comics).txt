The '''Schizoid Man''' is a fictional supervillain appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
The Schizoid Man first appeared in ''[[Spectacular Spider-Man]]'' Vol. 2 #36 and was created by [[Bill Mantlo]], [[John Romita, Jr.]] and [[Jim Mooney]]. 

==Fictional character biography==
'''Chip Martin''' is a graduate student at [[Empire State University]]<ref name="mnyc">{{cite book  | last = Sanderson  | first = Peter  | authorlink =   | coauthors =   | title = The Marvel Comics Guide to New York City  | publisher = [[Pocket Books]]  | date = 2007  | location = New York City  | pages = 30â€“33  | url =   | doi =   | id =   | isbn = 1-4165-3141-6}}</ref> who suffers from psychological instability and has the power of building and animating solid constructs with his mind. His father is Senator Robert Martin, a possible suspect of being the [[Hobgoblin (comics)|Hobgoblin]].<ref>''Spectacular Spider-Man'' (Vol. 2) #36</ref>

Schizoid Man joined Vil-Anon, a twelve-step program dedicated to helping individuals overcome their criminal tendencies which also consisted of [[Armadillo (comics)|Armadillo]], [[Equinox (comics)|Equinox]], [[Hypno-Hustler]], [[Big Wheel (comics)|Jackson Weele]] and [[Man-Bull]].<ref>''Spider-Man Unlimited'' #13</ref>

In ''[[Civil War (comic book)|Civil War]]: Battle Damage Report'', it is revealed that Chip and [[Lectronn]] engaged in a three-hour fight over New York that ended in a stalemate.<ref>''Civil War: Battle Damage Report'' #1</ref>

Schizoid Man was among several super-powered criminals housed in an unnamed ill-equipped prison in the aftermath of the ''[[Avengers Vs. X-Men]]'' storyline. [[Rogue (comics)|Rogue]] and [[Mimic (comics)|Mimic]] had to fight them off during a prison riot where Schizoid Man was trying to get control of himself.<ref>''X-Men Legacy'' #275.</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
The Schizoid Man possesses the power of building and animating solid constructs with his mind.

==Other versions==

===Ultimate Marvel===
The [[Ultimate Marvel]] version of Schizoid Man is an unnamed genetically-modified French citizen thanks to [[Jamie Madrox]]'s stolen stem cells. He uses his similar self-replication powers to control a riot before joining the [[Masters of Evil|Liberators]].<ref>''Ultimates'' Vol. 2 #9</ref> His team leads a large army to invade and conquer the United States, leading to the deaths of the [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] Giant-Man Reserves.<ref>''Ultimates'' Vol. 2 #10</ref> [[Ultimate Captain America|Captain America]] and the [[Wasp (comics)|Wasp]] defeat all of Schizoid Man's bodies that were "scattered all over the [[Triskelion (comics)|Triskelion]]".<ref>''Ultimates'' Vol. 2 #11</ref>
