'''Grindhouse''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. The character's first appearance was in ''[[Heroes for Hire]]'' vol. 2 #1.

==Fictional character biography==
Little is known of '''Grindhouse''''s activities before she was first seen,<ref>''Heroes for Hire'' vol. 2 #1</ref> however upon her first appearance she was tricked into a sting operation to seek a new identity from [[Vienna (comics)|Vienna]] and to escape the prosecution from the [[Superhero Registration Act]].{{issue|date=July 2013}}  Because of this set-up she was captured by the [[Heroes for Hire]].{{issue|date=July 2013}}
