'''Tremolo''' is a fictional [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Fictional character biography==
Tremolo was a member of female [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] terrorists. She was resuscitated by [[Cyber (Marvel Comics)|Cyber]] to commit acts of [[extortion]], and worked for a notorious [[drug cartel]]. Another member of group, [[Shrew (comics)|Shrew]] eventually quit the team and agreed to [[testify]] against the cartel for [[immunity from prosecution|immunity]].{{Issue|date=September 2009}} Tremolo and other The Belles, upon discovering their former teammate's betrayal, sought out to kill Shrew. The government then placed Shrew under the protection of [[X-Factor (comics)|X-Factor]].{{Issue|date=September 2009}} The Belles ambushed X-Factor in a hotel, and in the ensuing battle. She was initially driven off by Wolfsbane, and was later punched out by Quicksilver and taking them into custody with other defeated Belles.{{Issue|date=September 2009}}

She is currently depowered due to events of [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]].{{Issue|date=September 2009}}

==Powers and abilities==
Tremolo radiates sound and sonic waves around her body at all times, allowing her to fly, giving off explosive shock blasts, and vibro-shattering anything within range.
