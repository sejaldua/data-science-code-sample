'''Pasco''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''X-Men #191''.

==Fictional character biography==
Pasco and [[Sabretooth (comics)|Sabretooth]], were hired by Doctor Casales to assassinate people working on an experimental time acceleration project. They reached the tanker where the experiments were taking place, but Pasco lost his mutant powers on that day due to M-Day. The [[Children of the Vault]] emerged, and Pasco died in the resulting conflict.{{issue|date=January 2013}}

==Powers and abilities==
Pasco could create force fields.
