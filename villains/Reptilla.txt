'''Reptilla''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
Reptilla first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' vol. 1 #186 (September 1977), and was created by [[Len Wein]] and [[George Pérez]].

==Fictional character biography==
Reptilla is a daughter of [[Nicholas Scratch]] and is  a member of [[Salem's Seven]].

[[Wizard (Marvel Comics)|Wizard]] reformed the [[Frightful Four]] using [[Gazelle (Marvel Comics)|Gazelle]], Reptilla, and [[Vertigo (Salem's Seven)|Vertigo]] of [[Salem's Seven]], and they attacked Chicago to get the attention of [[Mister Fantastic]]. Mister Fantastic was almost defeated by the Frightful Four until [[Scarlet Witch]] appeared to help him.<ref>''[[Fantastic Four]]'' Vol. 5 #10</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Reptilla transforms into a [[snake]]-like form with a single long snake tail instead of legs and two poisonous snake instead of her arms. She has the ability to constrict and to also bite with her hand-snakes.
