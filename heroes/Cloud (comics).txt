'''Cloud''' is a [[fictional character|fictional]] superhero appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. The character is usually depicted in comics featuring the superhero team the [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]], of which Cloud has been a member.

==Publication history==
Cloud first appeared in ''[[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]]'' #123-124 (Septemberâ€“October 1983), and was created by [[J. M. DeMatteis]] and [[Don Perlin]]. She appeared as a regular member of the Defenders from that point on, in issues #127-152 (January 1984-February 1986) of the title. The character subsequently appeared in ''Solo Avengers'' #20 (July 1989), and ''Star Masters'' #1 (December 1995).

Cloud received an entry in the ''[[Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe Deluxe Edition]]'' #3.

==Fictional character biography==
Cloud is a sentient nebula, an immense cloud of gas, with the ability to assume human form. Cloud's initial human form was at first modeled after human female Carol Faber.<ref>''New Defenders'' #149 (November, 1985)</ref> After falling in love with [[Moondragon]], Cloud also developed a male form modeled after Danny Milligan.<ref>''Defenders'' #136 (October, 1984)</ref>

Cloud was an adventurer for a while with the [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]].<ref>''Defenders'' #127-139/''New Defenders'' #140-152</ref>

Cloud later traveled through space for a time with Moondragon, [[Sundragon]], and [[Gargoyle (comics)#Gargoyle (Isaac Christians)|Gargoyle]], and the [[Eternals (comics)|Eternal]] Demeityr, who had become Sundragon's lover.<ref>''Solo Avengers'' #20 (July, 1989)</ref>
