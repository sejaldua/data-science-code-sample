'''Captain Charlie-27''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. The character usually appears in the [[Earth-691]] timeline of the [[Marvel Universe]] as a member of the original 31st century incarnation of the team known as the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (1969 team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]].

Charlie-27 first appeared in ''[[Marvel Super-Heroes (comics)|Marvel Super-Heroes]]'' #18 (January 1969). Writer [[Dan Abnett]] described him as "the heart" of the Guardians of the Galaxy.<ref name="Newsarama2"/>

The character was portrayed by actor [[Ving Rhames]] in the 2017 [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] film ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]''.

==Fictional character biography==
Charlie-27 is a soldier and space militia pilot, genetically engineered to live in [[Jupiter]]'s gravity, born in Galileo City, Jupiter. As such, he has eleven times the muscle mass of a normal human. He was also a captain in the United Lands of Earth Space Militia.

In the year 3007, he returns from a six-month space mission to find his city taken over by the [[Badoon]]. He hears a Badoon mention he is the last surviving Jovian, so he escapes in a teleporting pod to Pluto. Unknown to him Starhawk had keyed in the location. He joins forces with [[Martinex]], the only survivor of Pluto. The two go to Earth using the telepod and meet [[Yondu]] and [[Vance Astro]] to form the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (1969 team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]], a band of freedom fighters.<ref>{{cite comic|writer=[[Arnold Drake|Drake, Arnold]]|penciller= [[Gene Colan|Colan, Gene]]|inker= [[Mike Esposito (comics)|Esposito, Mike]]|story= Guardians of the Galaxy!|title=[[Marvel Super-Heroes (comics)|Marvel Super-Heroes]] |issue=18|date=January 1969}}</ref> In 3014, he teams with the time-traveling [[Thing (comics)|Thing]], [[Captain America]], and [[Sharon Carter]] to retake New York City from Badoon forces.<ref>{{cite comic|writer=[[Steve Gerber|Gerber, Steve]]|penciller= [[Sal Buscema|Buscema, Sal]]|inker= Esposito, Mike|story= Seven Against the Empire!|title= [[Marvel Two-in-One]]|issue= 5|date= September 1974}}</ref> He later time-travels to the 20th Century, and meets the [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]]. He returns to 3015 with [[Starhawk (comics)|Starhawk]] and the Defenders to defeat a Badoon invasion force.<ref>{{cite comic|writer= Gerber, Steve; [[Gerry Conway|Conway, Gerry]]; [[Roger Slifer|Slifer, Roger]]; [[Len Wein|Wein, Len]]; [[Chris Claremont|Claremont, Chris]]; [[Scott Edelman|Edelman, Scott]]|penciller= [[Don Heck|Heck, Don]]|inker= [[Jim Mooney|Mooney, Jim]]; Esposito, Mike; Hunt, Dave|story= Eelar Moves in Mysterious Ways!|title= [[Defenders (comics)|Giant-Size Defenders]]|issue= 5|date= July 1975}}<br>{{cite comic|writer=Gerber, Steve|penciller=Buscema, Sal|inker=[[Vince Colletta|Colletta, Vince]]|story= Savage Time!|title=Defenders|issue=26|date=August 1975}}<br>{{cite comic|writer= Gerber, Steve|penciller= Buscema, Sal|inker= Colletta, Vince|story= Three Worlds to Conquer!|title=Defenders|issue=27|date=September 1975}}<br>{{cite comic|writer= Gerber, Steve|penciller= Buscema, Sal|inker= [[Frank Giacoia|Giacoia, Frank]]; [[John Tartaglione|Tartaglione, John]]|story=My Mother, The Badoon!|title= Defenders|issue=28|date=October 1975}}<br>{{cite comic|writer=Gerber, Steve |penciller= Buscema, Sal|inker= Colletta, Vince|story=Let My Planet Go!|title= Defenders|issue= 29|date= November 1975}}</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Charlie-27 is a member of a genetically engineered offshoot of humanity whose traits were designed for survival under the heavy gravity conditions of the planet Jupiter. As such, he has superhuman strength, and enhanced durability and stamina.

Charlie is trained in hand-to-hand combat as a militiaman. He also has the ability to pilot most air and spacecraft of the 31st Century, including the Guardians' starships such as the ''[[Freedom's Lady]]'', ''Captain America I'', and ''Captain America II''.
